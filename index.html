Real-data training path

Designed for SCIN and Fitzpatrick17k.

SCIN

Real-world skin images

Google Research's SCIN dataset contains user-contributed dermatology images with dermatologist labels and skin-tone metadata. Filter face/head locations only.

Fitzpatrick17k

Clinical images and skin type

A public dermatology benchmark with Fitzpatrick skin type annotations. Use only face-relevant classes/images after manual filtering.

Safety boundary

Screening support. Not diagnosis.
